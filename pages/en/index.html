Your STRENGTH and SAFETY a touch away

We believe in the power of information and unity to create a safer environment for everyone. "Elas por Elas" is your digital ally, offering essential protection resources and a support community at your fingertips. Together, we are stronger.

Here you are protected

Elas por Elas was created with a single purpose: to ensure your safety. Here you will find a welcoming space, made to listen, support, and guide. Every feature was designed for you to feel safe and supported in any situation.

Find police stations near you

Easily find the police stations and support centers closest to your location. Our geolocation system helps you identify where to seek in-person help quickly and safely, always prioritizing your protection.

Report anonymously if you don't feel comfortable

You have the right to report without exposing yourself. If you prefer to keep your identity confidential, we offer the option of anonymous reporting. This way, you contribute to combating violence without putting your safety at risk.
